How to Play: Minesweeper-Like Game

🎯 Objective

Reveal as many safe tiles (💎 gems) as possible without hitting a bomb (💣). Each gem increases your winnings. Cash out before you hit a bomb to secure your earnings!

🕹️ How It Works

  • Enter your Bet Amount.
  • Select how many Bombs you want on the grid (more bombs = higher risk, higher reward).
  • Click "Start Game" to generate the grid and begin playing.
  • Click tiles to reveal 💎 or 💣.
  • Each 💎 found increases your Current Winnings.
  • You can click "Cash Out" at any time to secure your winnings.
  • If you hit a 💣, you lose the bet.

💡 Tips

  • Start with fewer bombs if you're new.
  • More bombs = higher multiplier but also more risk!
  • Use the "Cash Out" button wisely — don't get too greedy!

📊 Multiplier System

The multiplier increases based on the number of bombs and revealed gems. Revealing more gems without hitting a bomb increases the winnings exponentially.

🎮 How to Play: Slot Machine

  1. Check Your Balance: Your available balance is displayed at the top. Make sure you have enough funds to place a bet.
  2. Place a Bet: Enter the amount you wish to bet in the input box.
  3. Spin the Reels: Click the "Spin" button to spin the reels.
  4. Wait for the Result: The reels will spin and then reveal 3 random symbols.
  5. Win Conditions:
    • 🎉 3 Matching Symbols: Big win! The payout is based on the symbol's multiplier.
    • ✨ 2 Matching Symbols: You win 1.5x your bet.
    • ❌ No Matches: Better luck next time!
  6. Payouts by Symbol:
    • 🍒 = 2x
    • 🍋 = 3x
    • 🍉 = 4x
    • ⭐ = 6x
    • 7️⃣ = 10x (Jackpot!)
  7. Sound Effects: Each result is accompanied by a win, small win, or loss sound to enhance the experience.
  8. Repeat & Enjoy: Adjust your bet and try your luck again!
